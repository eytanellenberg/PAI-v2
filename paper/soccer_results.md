## Data sources

Soccer analyses are based on public event-level match data,
including passes, shots, duels, pressures, and playing time.

Event data allow fine-grained reconstruction of
individual involvement and contextual match dynamics.

---

## Performance outcomes

Performance outcomes reflect contribution to team danger creation
rather than isolated actions.

---

## Attribution focus

Attribution distinguishes between:
- technical execution
- tactical role and positioning
- contextual match constraints
